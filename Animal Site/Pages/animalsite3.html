Crows

Intelligent birds known for their problem-solving abilities and social behavior.

    Crow Features:
  1. Highly intelligent and capable of problem-solving
  2. Have a complex social structure and communicate with each other
  3. Can use tools to obtain food
  4. Have a varied diet, including insects, small animals, and plant matter
  5. Known for their adaptability to different environments
    Crow Habitat:
  • Found in a wide range of habitats, including forests, grasslands, and urban areas
  • Can adapt to various climates and environments
  • Nests in trees, shrubs, and man-made structures
  • Often found near water sources for drinking and bathing
