Peregrine Falcon

A type of falcon that can reach very high speeds.

    Features of the Peregrine Falcon:
  1. Fastest animal in the world
  2. Can reach speeds of over 200 mph
  3. Has a wingspan of 3.3 to 3.6 feet
  4. Has a sharp beak and talons for hunting
  5. Has excellent eyesight for spotting prey from a distance
  6. Eats other birds, especially ducks and geese
    Habitat of the Peregrine Falcon:
  • Found on every continent except Antarctica
  • Prefers open landscapes and coastal areas
  • Nests on cliffs, tall buildings, and bridges
  • Can adapt to urban environments
  • Often found near water sources for hunting
  • Some populations have been reintroduced to areas where they were previously extinct
